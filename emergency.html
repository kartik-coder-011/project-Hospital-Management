First Aid Guide

×

Cardiac Arrest

  • Call emergency services immediately
  • Start CPR: 30 chest compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths
  • Use AED if available
  • Continue until help arrives

Severe Bleeding

  • Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
  • Elevate the injured area above heart if possible
  • Apply pressure bandage
  • Do not remove embedded objects

Burns

  • Cool burn with running cool water for 10-15 minutes
  • Cover with sterile gauze or clean cloth
  • Do not apply ice, butter, or ointments
  • Seek medical attention for severe burns

Fractures

  • Immobilize the injured area
  • Apply cold pack to reduce swelling
  • Do not try to realign bones
  • Seek immediate medical help
